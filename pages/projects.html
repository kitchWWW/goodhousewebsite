Projects.

Karakurenai - 2018
"Karakurenai" by Andy Akiho (Japanese for "foreign crimson") was originally written for solo prepared steel pan in June 2007 as part of the Synesthesia Suite; however, this piece can be performed on any combination of instruments and can include elements of improvisation if the performer desires.


Unset - 2018
Unset 2.0 is the product of a collaboration initiated by The Interactive Deep Dive, a think tank devoted to developing interactive story techniques applied across mediums. With the collaborators here at Deep Dive Austin, the Good House Collective works towards the goal of prototyping and developing interactive story projects.

Time Warp - 2017
What happens when the present meets the past? In our first ever Good House Concert, we explore what great works of old and new have in common. Works by Mozart, Bach, Reich, Earth, Wind & Fire, as well as two world premiers.
